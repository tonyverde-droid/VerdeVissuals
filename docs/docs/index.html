About Diego

Diego Verde started VerdeVissuals out of a simple love for sports and a camera he taught himself to use on the sidelines of his own games.

What began as a way to capture moments for friends and teammates has grown into a photography business serving youth athletes, teams, couples, and individuals across South Florida.

Every shoot is approached with the same goal: preserve memories that last long after the final whistle.
